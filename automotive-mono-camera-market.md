<p><strong>The Automotive Mono Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mono camera market is primarily categorized into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras offer high image quality and low noise, making them suitable for low-light conditions, but are generally more power-consuming and expensive. Conversely, CMOS cameras are favored for their lower power consumption, reduced cost, and faster processing capabilities, making them ideal for advanced driver-assistance systems and real-time applications. The preference for CMOS is increasing, driving growth in the automotive sector.</p></p>
