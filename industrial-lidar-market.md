<p><strong>The Industrial LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial LiDAR market comprises two primary types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes a fixed array of sensors without moving parts, offering durability and compactness, ideal for environments with limited space. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture a broader field of view, providing detailed 3D mapping capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing factors like cost, accuracy, and maintenance, making them suitable for various industrial uses such as automation and surveying.</p></p>
