<p><p>Residential connected lighting encompasses various technologies like WiFi, ZigBee, Z-Wave, Bluetooth, and Thread. WiFi allows direct internet connectivity for control via smartphones. ZigBee and Z-Wave are low-power, mesh networking protocols ideal for smart home integration and energy efficiency. Bluetooth offers easy pairing for short-range connectivity, suitable for personal environments. Thread is a newer, low-power mesh protocol focusing on secure device communication. Each technology caters to specific user needs, enhancing convenience and automation in smart lighting systems.</p></p>
