<p><strong>The RF Feeder Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coax or Coaxial Cable</li><li>Open Wire or Twin Feeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF feeder cables market comprises primarily two types: coaxial cables and open wire (twin feeder) systems. Coaxial cables are widely used due to their ability to effectively shield against electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for various radio frequency applications. They feature a central conductor surrounded by an insulating layer and an outer conductive shield. Open wire systems, on the other hand, consist of parallel conductors and are typically employed in high-frequency applications, offering lower losses but requiring careful installation to maintain performance.</p></p>
