<p><strong>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe System</li><li>Dry Pipe System</li><li>Deluge System</li><li>Preaction System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water mist sprinkler market encompasses various types of systems tailored for fire suppression. The wet pipe system uses pressurized water ready for immediate discharge. The dry pipe system, ideal for freezing conditions, holds water in a remote location, releasing it upon activation. The deluge system features open sprinkler heads, flooding the area with water upon detection of fire. Lastly, the preaction system combines elements of the wet and dry systems, requiring detection before water is released to minimize accidental discharge.</p></p>
