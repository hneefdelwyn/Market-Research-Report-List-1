<p><p>The palletizing robots market is categorized into two main types: 2D vision and 3D vision palletizing robots. 2D vision palletizing robots use flat images to identify and position products for stacking, relying on standard cameras for object detection. In contrast, 3D vision palletizing robots utilize advanced depth-sensing technology to understand the spatial arrangement of items, enhancing accuracy and flexibility in handling various shapes and sizes. Both types aim to improve efficiency and productivity in automated packaging and warehousing operations.</p></p>
