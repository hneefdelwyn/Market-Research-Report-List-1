<p><strong>The Drinking-water Fountains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BWC</li><li>POU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking-water fountains market is primarily divided into two types: Bottled Water Coolers (BWC) and Point of Use (POU) systems. BWC units dispense water from pre-packaged bottles, ideal for locations with limited plumbing infrastructure. In contrast, POU systems connect directly to existing water lines, providing a continual supply of filtered water on demand, making them more sustainable and cost-effective over time. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, from convenience to efficiency.</p></p>
