<p><strong>The Point-of-Use Water Heaters (POU Water Heaters) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tank Point-of-Use Water Heaters</li><li>Tankless Point-of-Use Water Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point-of-Use (POU) water heaters are installed close to the water outlet, providing hot water on demand and reducing the need for long pipe runs. The market is divided into two main types: tank POU water heaters, which store a limited volume of hot water for immediate use, and tankless POU water heaters, which heat water directly as it flows through the unit. Tankless models are often favored for their energy efficiency and unlimited hot water supply, while tank models are typically more cost-effective for smaller applications.</p></p>
