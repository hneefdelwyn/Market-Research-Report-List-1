<p><strong>The MEMS LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1D MEMS LIDAR</li><li>2D MEMS LIDAR</li><li>3D MEMS LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS LiDAR market encompasses three main types: 1D, 2D, and 3D LiDAR systems. 1D MEMS LiDAR focuses on single-axis scanning, typically used for distance measurement. 2D MEMS LiDAR expands this capability to two-dimensional scanning, providing planar mapping and object detection. In contrast, 3D MEMS LiDAR generates detailed three-dimensional representations of environments, making it suitable for advanced applications like autonomous vehicles and robotics, where spatial awareness and obstacle detection are critical for navigation and decision-making.</p></p>
