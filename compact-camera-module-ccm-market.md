<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market consists of two primary types: Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for professional applications. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer advantages like lower power consumption, smaller size, and faster processing speeds, making them widely used in smartphones and consumer electronics. The growth of the CCM market is driven by advancements in image processing technology and increasing demand for high-quality imaging solutions.</p></p>
