<p><strong>The DC Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop Current Sensors</li><li>Open Loop Current Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC current sensors market comprises two main types: closed-loop and open-loop current sensors. Closed-loop current sensors utilize feedback mechanisms to provide high accuracy and stability, making them ideal for precise applications. They often incorporate a magnetic core and can handle varying loads effectively. In contrast, open-loop current sensors measure current without feedback, offering simpler designs and lower costs, but with less precision. Both types cater to diverse applications, including industrial automation, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
