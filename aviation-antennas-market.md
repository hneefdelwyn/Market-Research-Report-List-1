<p><ul><li>Directional Antenna</li><li>Omnidirectional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation antennas market comprises primarily two types: directional and omnidirectional antennas. Directional antennas are designed to focus their signal in a specific direction, enhancing communication range and signal strength for targeted applications, such as aircraft navigation or air traffic control. In contrast, omnidirectional antennas provide 360-degree signal coverage, making them ideal for general communication needs within an airport or for maintaining contact during flight. Both types are crucial for ensuring reliable communication and navigation in aviation operations.</p></p>
