<p><p>The Static Cone Penetrometer market is primarily divided into two types: Hand Operated and Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometers. Hand Operated Static Cone Penetrometers rely on manual effort for penetration into the ground, making them ideal for small-scale projects with limited access. In contrast, Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometers feature automated mechanisms that allow for consistent and efficient data collection in a variety of soil conditions, making them suitable for larger, more complex geotechnical investigations. Both types are essential for assessing soil properties.</p></p>
