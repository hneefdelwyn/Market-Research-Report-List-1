<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor digital mass flow controller market is primarily categorized into two types: thermal and pressure types. Thermal mass flow controllers operate by measuring the heat transfer from a heated element to the fluid, providing accurate flow measurements based on temperature changes. In contrast, pressure type mass flow controllers function by assessing the pressure drop across a flow restriction, which correlates to the flow rate. Both types are essential for precise flow control in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
