<p><p>The coding equipment market is segmented into several types based on technology and application. Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) printers are widely used for high-speed coding on various surfaces. Thermal Inkjet (TIJ) printers offer precise, high-quality markings, suitable for smaller applications. Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) is effective for flexible packaging, providing durable prints. Laser coders offer permanent marking with high precision, ideal for products requiring long-lasting identification. Other methods include label printers and dot matrix printers, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
