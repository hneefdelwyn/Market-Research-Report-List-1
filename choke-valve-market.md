<p><ul><li>Straight-through Type</li><li>Angle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The choke valve market consists of two primary types: straight-through and angle types. Straight-through choke valves allow fluid flow in a linear direction, minimizing pressure drops and enhancing flow efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, angle-type choke valves divert fluid flow at an angle, which can help optimize space and simplify installation in constrained setups. Both designs cater to various industrial applications, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and water management, focusing on flow regulation and control.</p></p>
