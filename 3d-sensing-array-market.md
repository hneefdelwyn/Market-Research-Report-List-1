<p><strong>The 3D Sensing Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Sensing Array market encompasses two primary types: Structured Light and Time of Flight. Structured Light technology projects a known pattern onto the subject and captures the distortion to calculate depth and shape. This method is commonly used in applications like facial recognition and augmented reality. Conversely, Time of Flight measures the time it takes for a light signal to travel to an object and back, offering precise distance measurements, making it ideal for autonomous vehicles and robotics.</p></p>
