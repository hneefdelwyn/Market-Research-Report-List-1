<p><ul><li>Jet & Venturi Scrubbers</li><li>Orifice Scrubbers</li><li>Packed Bed Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas scrubbing systems are essential for removing pollutants from gas streams across various industries. Jet and Venturi scrubbers use high-velocity jets to create turbulence, efficiently capturing particles and impurities. Orifice scrubbers operate by forcing gas through orifices, promoting contact with scrubbing liquids to facilitate pollution removal. Packed bed scrubbers consist of a tower filled with packing material that enhances gas-liquid interaction, effectively absorbing contaminants. Each type has unique applications, efficiency levels, and operational characteristics tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
