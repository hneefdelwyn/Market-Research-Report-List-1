<p><p>The instant beverage premix market encompasses three primary types: powder, paste, and granules. Powder premixes are dehydrated blends that dissolve quickly in water, ideal for making drinks like coffee and tea. Paste premixes offer a concentrated form, requiring less water and providing a richer flavor, often used in specialty beverages. Granules, on the other hand, are larger particles that dissolve at a moderate pace, commonly used for strong-flavored drinks. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and preparation methods.</p></p>
